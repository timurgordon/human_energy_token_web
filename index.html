1 HOUR OF LABOUR = 1 HUMAN ENERGY TOKEN

A Token of Equality

The Human Energy Token ensures that every person who puts the same amount of time aside to help another is rewareded equally.

A Token of Appreciation

The human energy token is a way of saying "thank you for your time and effort" as it bases its value on time and effort.

A Token of Sustainability

The human energy token ensures a sustainable economy through a value backed peer-to-peer token. It is as resilient as time itself.

HOW IT WORKS

Make a request for a labour-based service

Receive service from a merchant/freelancer

Acknowledge his time and effort with HETs
